HOW TO PLAY:
 * USE THE DIRECTIONAL KEYS or
 * USE THE MOUSE - DRAG HOLDING LMB (click & drag) or
 * USE THE LEFT STICK OF A GAMEPAD

Beat the mouse before it eats all the food.
Drive the mouse into traps to get points or catch up to the mouse and take a bit of its hp off.
When the mouse's hp reaches 0 you win.
If the mouse eats all the food you lose.

The game probably still has some bugs left but try to have fun.
